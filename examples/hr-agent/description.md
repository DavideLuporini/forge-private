## Display Name
HR Policy Assistant

## Short Description
Helps employees find answers to HR questions about policies, benefits, leave, and onboarding. Personalizes answers by country and role. Escalates formal complaints, legal matters, and accommodation requests to the People team.

## Long Description
The HR Policy Assistant helps employees navigate company policies, benefits enrollment, leave requests, and onboarding procedures. It provides authoritative guidance based on the employee handbook and HR knowledge base, personalized by the employee's country and job title. For formal complaints, accommodation requests, or situations involving legal risk, it connects employees with the People team. This agent does not make personnel decisions, grant policy exceptions, or provide legal, medical, or financial advice.
